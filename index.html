Installation Guide

  1. Upload your .saber file from Beat Saber custom sabers
  2. Click "CONVERT" and wait for processing
  3. Download the generated ZIP file
  4. Extract the ZIP contents to Beat Saber/UserData/ReeSabers/
  5. Launch Beat Saber and load your saber in ReeSabers mod
