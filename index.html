> Current Limitations & Notes

  • Manual Tweaking: You will likely need to edit the generated presets to get them perfect. Simpler sabers require much less work.
  • Positioning: Due to how UnityPy unpacks assets, models will only be perfectly positioned if the original creator centered everything at 0,0,0.
  • No Motion Blur: ReeSabers does not currently support motion blur on custom models, so this feature is unavailable.
  • Success Rate: While some sabers convert perfectly to their original look, results vary based on the original file's complexity.

> How to Use

  1. Upload a legacy .saber file.
  2. Click CONVERT and download the resulting ZIP.
  3. Extract contents to Beat Saber/UserData/ReeSabers/.
  4. Load the preset via the ReeSabers mod in-game.
